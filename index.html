Fractal Trees

Fractal Trees is a generative art NFT collection inspired to fractals, generated by the union of hand painted images and coding. Blockchain and code have a strong connection with the outside world and art, nature around us is able to generate configurations that we have to preserve. The patterns and shapes that nature offers to humanity are a spurce of inspiration in artistic creation.

Trees are the protagonists of FRACTAL TREES as they are like natural fractals, characterised by patterns that repeat smaller and smaller copies of themselves to create the biodiversity of a forest. Each tree branch, from the trunk to the top, is generated by the one that came before it. For us fractals are a common artistic language that connect us and nature.
